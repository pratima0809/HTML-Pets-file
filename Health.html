Health

Origins

A UK study found a life expectancy of 12.2 years for the Pomeranian.
A Japanese study of pet cemetery data found a life expectancy of 14 years,
below the average of 15.3 years for cross-breed dogs below 10kgs.
Pomeranians are one of the breeds with the smallest average litter size,
with various sources giving numbers of between 1.9 and 2.7 puppies per litter.
