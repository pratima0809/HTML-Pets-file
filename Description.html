Description

The Pomeranian (also known as a Pom, Pommy or Pome)
is a breed of dog of the Spitz type that is named for the
Pomerania region in north-west Poland and north-east Germany
in Central Europe. Classed as a toy dog breed because of its small
size, the Pomeranian is descended from larger Spitz-type dogs,
specifically the German Spitz.
Additionally, they are prized as sporting and hunting dogs.
